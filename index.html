A bit about me

I am 26 years old, and I live in sunny San Diego, California. I currently work in property management as a leasing consultant and thought this was a field that I would like to pursue. I am always thinking of ways to challenge myself intellectually and financially. I find myself bored at my desk job where I spend 8 hours a day, 40 hours a week and find myself anxious for something different.

Software engineering was something I'd never thought I would see myself in, nor was it something that I thought I was capable of doing.. but I was wrong. Coding has given me fullfillment in being able to challenge myself intellectually and express my creativity.
